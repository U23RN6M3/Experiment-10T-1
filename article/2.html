Reality Breaker 001, who captures rogue reality breakers and maintains the order of the multiverse, has been lost in time and space. The Facility, QUFA, created entities to replace the reality breaker. They were U23RN2M3, U23RN3M3, and so on until U23RN6M3.

U23RN6M3 was the final result of all the past prototype bioweapons. It was classified as a wild reality breaker by the QUFA supreme board for unknown reasons.

U23RN6M3 was supposed to be transfered to QUFA HQ where it will be given reality breaking powers. Where it will be given the title of the most powerful reality breaker in the whole multiverse.
